 *University President · Senior Pastor · Global Women's Leader · Author · Pharmacist*

Purpose:

- Position Dr. Toyin Tofade as a thought leader across academia, healthcare, and faith. 

Goals:

- Create a website for Dr. Toyin Tofade that highlights her legacy and impact   
- Increase speaking engagement requests  
- Establish thought leadership  
- Inspire women (especially Black women in leadership)

Target Audiences: 

- Academic audiences  
  - ACPHS students, faculty, donors, prospective students, higher ed peers, global pharmacy organizations  
- Faith community  
  - JCCI members in Chapel of Victory and other church branches  
  - Global Women Leaders community  
- Broader public  
  - Nigerian diaspora, Black women in leadership, media, organizations seeking board members  
- Conference organizers  
  - Booking for discussions about pharmacy, DEI, leadership, higher education, women’s ministries
